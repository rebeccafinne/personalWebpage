Bare Necessities

“Bare Necessities” is a website for students in the ages 13 to 15. The main goal with “Bare Necessities” is to educate students in how privileged they are and that not all children have the same prerequisites as they have. The bare necessities in this case is clean water, enough food, access to shelter, and the ability to read and write.
