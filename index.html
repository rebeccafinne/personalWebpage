Who am I?

My name is Rebecca Finne, I'm 24 years old and I'm an Interaction design student with a bachelor in Software engineering at Chalmers University of Technology, in Gothenburg Sweden. I plan for my studies to be finished in the spring of 2020.

What I dream about in my future job is to be able to make a difference, in some way. It could be anything from ending poverty to an individual having an simpler everyday life though any product that I have been a part of creating. This will be possible for me because of my academic background and my experience from previous projects.

Bare Necessities

“Bare Necessities” is a website for students in the ages 13 to 15. The main goal with “Bare Necessities” is to educate students in how privileged they are and that not all children have the same prerequisites as they have. The bare necessities in this case is clean water, enough food, access to shelter, and the ability to read and write.
