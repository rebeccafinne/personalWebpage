Who am I?

My name is Rebecca Finne, I'm 24 years old and I'm a Software engineering student currently studying my master in Interaction Design at Chalmers University of Technology, in Gothenburg Sweden. I plan for my studies to be finished in the spring of 2020.

What I dream about in my future job is to be able to, in some way, make a difference. It could be anything to end poverty to an individual having an easier everyday life though any product that I have been a part of creating and developing. With my academical background this will be possible for me, by together with a team develop a great product.

Below you can see some of my projects that I have been a part of.

Portfolio
Ses

Developed a meet-up organizer application called Ses. The application supports setting your status as available or busy and finding events close by. The application is developed in React Native and I was mostly responsible for the look and feel of the application.

Room Booking System

School project where I designed a room booking system for a university. The design consist of two versions, one for teachers and one for students. For this design I followed a few requrements.
This is made in Sketch.

Bechelor's Thesis: A workout application with focus on user ecperience

An application made in Android, called BetterTogether. The user do different workouts to earn points and every week compete against friends and family. I did different things in both back-end, front-end and UX, tools we used was for example the data base Firebase, Sketch and XML.

IMPACT

At the #MeToo hack in Gothenburg 2018, my team made an Android application with tips of what to do in different situations where sexual harrasement occur.

SvenScan

An Android application for refugees learning Swedish. They scan a word with the camera on the phone and a picture representing the word shows up and at the same time an audio of the word is played.

Chalmers Run and Play

A top down desktop application where the user plays a character and are supposed to catch animals.
